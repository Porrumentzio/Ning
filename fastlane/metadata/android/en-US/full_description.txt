Scan your local network for connected devices. Inspired by Fing.

The goal is to provide a quick and easy overview over the local network.

Data sources include:
* Ping devices
* Network Service Discovery (Bonjour, Avahi)
* ARP-Tables
* open TCP and UDP ports
